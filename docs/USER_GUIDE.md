# User Guide: Dadi Translator

## What Dadi Translator Does

Dadi Translator converts Simplified Chinese documents into professional English documents. It is designed for internal company use, especially for business, education, operations, and management documents.

## Supported Uploads

You may upload:

- Word document: `.docx`
- Legacy Word document: `.doc`
- Text file: `.txt`
- Markdown file: `.md`

For best results, use `.docx`, `.txt`, or `.md`.

## How to Use the App

### Step 1: Open Dadi Translator

Open the deployed Streamlit app link.

### Step 2: Upload Your Chinese Document

Click the upload area and select your file.

### Step 3: Review Extracted Chinese Text

After upload, the app will show how many characters were extracted. You may open the preview section to check the Chinese text.

### Step 4: Translate

Click **Translate to English**.

Wait until the translation process is complete.

### Step 5: Review and Edit

The translated English content will appear in an editable text box. You may revise the title or body text before downloading.

### Step 6: Download Word Document

Click **Download Word Document**.

The generated file name will use the translated English title.

## Recommended Document Preparation

Before uploading, make sure your Chinese document has:

- A clear title on the first line
- Clear section numbering
- Clean paragraph spacing
- No unnecessary repeated headers or footers
- No scanned image-only text

## Output Format

The downloaded Word document includes:

- Dadi Coach logo in the header
- Centered English title
- One-column English translation
- Professional paragraph spacing
- Preserved section order and bullet structure

## Important Notes

- The app translates text-based documents only.
- It does not read text inside images.
- Scanned PDFs or photo-based documents are not supported in the first version.
- Always review the translation before sharing externally.
